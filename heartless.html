Heartless

What is a Heartless?

Heartless are physical, living manifestations of the darkness within people's hearts.

They manifest in two forms; "Pureblood" and the artificially created "Emblem" Heartless.

They behave entirely devoid of emotion, hence the name "Heartless".

Pureblood

The Pureblood Heartless are natural Heartless, born when people's hearts are normally consumed by darkness.

They are ink-black in color, with yellow beady eyes; though they start taking on additional colors the bigger they are.

They are more common in places that are close to or saturated in darkness.

Emblem

The Emblem Heartless are Heartless created in Ansem's Lab.

They were created by Xehanort using the Pureblood as synthesizing material.

They are usually more powerful than normal Heartless and they all bear the Heartless Emblem.
