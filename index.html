Kingdom Hearts 2


What is Kingdom Hearts 2?

Kingdom Hearts II is an action role-playing game developed and published by Square Enix and released in 2005.

It is the sequel to Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories,

and like the two previous games, focuses on Sora and his friends' continued battle against the Darkness.

Kingdom Hearts 2 also had another version released which was called Kingdom Hearts 2: Final Mix

which was released as the definitive edition of the game.

Kingdom Hearts 2: Final Mix added new features into the game such as a new form for sora and a new difficulty

and a total of 14 new super bosses which are located in a new area that was added to this version.
