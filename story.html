Story

One year after the events of Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, Sora and his friends awaken in Twilight Town.

Bent on his quest to find Riku and King Mickey Mouse the three set off on another adventure.

However, they soon discover that while they have been asleep, the Heartless are back and are once again causing problems for the worlds

Not only that, but Sora, Donald, and Goofy must face new enemies: The Nobodies and Organization 13.

They set off on a quest to rid the world of the Heartless once more, uncovering the many secrets that linger about Ansem and Organization 13.
